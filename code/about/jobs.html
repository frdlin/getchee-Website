Jobs

We're always looking for talented, fun folks to join our team. getchee is an incredible place to work, learn, share, and grow.

getchee is the leading solution provider in Asia Pacific assisting business leaders mitigate risk and make better business decisions.  We make software and data that helps global brands expand and optimize in emerging markets throughout China and Southeast Asia.

Our global software solutions, data, and services provide our customers with a greater competitive advantage through our diverse offerings geared towards making spatial data useful for better business decisions.

We're a young company filled with energetic, bright people. We are fortunate to have leaders within our organization that are playful, yet professional.

The people at getchee are positive and supportive to our vision of providing the best service and value to our clients as possible.

Business Analyst

Primary job role is to assist in the collection of business requirements from customers in the retail industry, and provide suggestions for improvements to their business processes.

You'll gain valuable on-the-job experience while getting immersed in our collaborative, fun, and engaging culture.

This position will involve the analysis and documentation of client business processes.

Although you will be provided guidance and a basic set of goals, we expect all employees to be independent thinkers, and provide their own input to the team.

Candidates must have the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

Education — You should be a degree holder with at least 5 years of experience in the retail industry.

Experience — You should have experience defining technical SDLC requirements using a variety of graphical and textual techniques, including use case scenarios, diagrams, functional requirements specifications, activity and process diagrams, and data dictionaries. Experience with Requisite PRO, Rational Rose, or related products is a plus.

Passion — You should be passionate about learning new technologies.

To apply for this position, please send your resume to jobs@getchee.com, and we'll arrange an interview.

Responsibilities
  • Provide business consulting service for customers in the retail industry.
  • Analyze customers' business process and definition of requirements.
  • Provide support to customers on application implementation.
  • Define and identify scope changes during the project lifecycle.
Qualifications
  • Have a familiarity with MS Office.
  • Strong analytical, problem identification and solving skills.
  • Understanding of database and software design techniques and diagramming.
  • Open-minded, innovative, collaborative, and team-oriented.
  • Good written and verbal skills.

Data Operations Assistant

Assist in the development and management of data content that powers getchee software, services, and solutions.

You'll gain valuable on-the-job experience while getting immersed in our collaborative, fun, and engaging culture.

This position will be responsible for the collection of key brand data, including geolocation, brand mix, and brand attributes. Knowledge of how to create business modeling for population, intensity, and wealth indication is necessary.

Although you will be provided guidance and a basic set of goals, we expect all employees to be independent thinkers, and provide their own input to the team.

Candidates must have the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

Education — You should be a college graduate with a degree in geography, urban planning, GIS, or environmental engineering.

Experience — You should have some work or academic experience that demonstrates teamwork and collaboration.

Passion — You should be passionate about learning new technologies.

To apply for this position, please send your resume to jobs@getchee.com, and we'll arrange an interview.

Responsibilities
  • Work with project team to understand data-related scope.
  • Assist in processing project related tasks.
  • Manage internal data warehouse.
  • Support pre-sales and marketing activities.
Qualifications
  • Have a familiarity with MS Office.
  • 2 years experience using ESRI ArcGIS.
  • Open-minded, innovative, collaborative, and team-oriented.
  • Familiar with intensity modeling.
  • Familiar with demographic modeling.
  • Knowledge of data operation processes.

Sales Support Specialist

Work with getchee’s sales and marketing teams to research market information, identify potential sales leads, arrange sales meetings, plan and coordinate events, and help develop sales and marketing material.

You’ll gain valuable on-the-job experience while getting immersed in our collaborative, fun, and engaging culture.

This position will be a key member of getchee’s sales and marketing team, and will work with colleagues based in both Shanghai and Taipei. The focus work will be on retailers, food and beverage, and apparel brands operating in the China market.

Job tasks will include research in several areas, including market trends, target client information, and competitor activities. This person will also identify potential business leads, arrange and attend sales meetings, and support presentation and proposal writing.

Other areas of activity include planning and coordinating market events, and helping the marketing team to develop printed, web-based, and social media marketing content.

Although you will be provided guidance and a basic set of goals, we expect all employees to be independent thinkers, and provide their own input to the team.

Candidates must have the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

Education — You should be a college graduate with a bachelor's degree.

Experience — You should have 2-3 years of enterprise work experience. You should also have strong research, analysis, and presentation abilities.

Passion — You should be passionate about learning new technologies.

To apply for this position, please send your resume to jobs@getchee.com, and we'll arrange an interview.

Responsibilities
  • Perform market research
  • Identify new business leads
  • Write presentation material and create new project proposals
  • Plan and organizing marketing events
Qualifications
  • Have a familiarity with MS Office.
  • Open-minded, innovative, collaborative, and team-oriented.
  • Excellent writing and speaking skills in both Chinese and English
  • Outgoing and friendly personality

Senior Project Manager

Work within the project management team to field and organize customer requests, ensure project deliverables, deadlines, and goals are met, and to oversee the general delivery of project outputs.

In this position, you will lead a client service team to manage and service a group of clients in order to maintain and grow clientele base so as to achieve planned financial objectives and other operational performance indicators.

This position will require the need to maintain and develop relationships with current and new clients. It will demand a need for efficient project management to ensure revenue goals are met and the speed and quality of deliverables are accomplished.

Although you will be provided guidance and a basic set of goals, we expect all employees to be independent thinkers, and provide their own input to the team.

Candidates must have the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

Education — You should be a college graduate with a BA/BS (MBA desirable).

Experience — You should have 2-3 years of enterprise work experience. You should also have strong research, analysis, and presentation abilities.

Passion — You should be passionate about learning new technologies.

To apply for this position, please send your resume to jobs@getchee.com, and we'll arrange an interview.

Responsibilities
  • Maintain and develop relationships with current and new clients
  • Achieve revenue, profitability, quality, speed, and productivity targets
  • Oversee final output for projects including reports, proposals, and presentations
Qualifications
  • Over 5 years financial, service, or retail industry experience
  • 3 years in a management position
  • Strong client-oriented focus
  • Expert knowledge of statistics and multivariate analysis
