Why can’t I connect even though I meet the minimum requirements?

  • Your firewall might be blocking access. Kiwi™; uses 128-bit security known as SSL. This secure service requires port 443 to be open, or unblocked. If you are behind a firewall that blocks this port, you will not be able to access the system. Please contact your IT department to open this port for use.
  •  
  • If you have confirmed this port is open and still have problems, please contact getchee at support@getchee.com for further assistance.

Why is the connection speed slow?

  • In order for smooth performance and speed, you need a transfer rate of at least 50 kilobytes per second. Find out what your connection speed is to our server here.
  •  
  • If you fall below 50kb/second, we may still be able to help you. Send an email to support@getchee.com for further assistance.

Why don’t I see a point of interest that I know exists?

  • We try and collect point of interests (POI) that are standard to most location-based analysis. If you don’t see a POI that you need, just ask us. Our data folks are very good at cultivating new and custom data.
  •  

Is it possible to add additional data into the tool?

  • Yes, it is possible to add additional value-added data and customized datasets. For more information regarding getchee value-added data, please see our data list. If you are interested in acquiring a customized dataset, please send an email to support@getchee.com for further assistance.

How accurate is getchee Data™ and where does it come from?

  • Data available in economically-emerging regions is never 100% accurate. This is a reality of any rapidly changing market landscape. At getchee, we use our market and analyses skills to test data models. These models aren’t perfect but they increase your data accuracy to the 85-90% levels. Since, most data in the region starts at 60% of less, we think we are on the right track.
  •  

What is getchee value-added data?

  • Value-added data consists of modeled datasets using getchee proprietary logic. These datasets include, but are not limited to: daytime population, working population and wealth indication.

About Kiwi™

Kiwi™ is a location-based Web application that helps managers with better site planning, site management, site optimization, and marketing efforts in emerging markets. Kiwi™ is supported by standardized current market data.
