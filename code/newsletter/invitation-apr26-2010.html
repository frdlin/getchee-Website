Wuhan Trade Area Case Study

 

Urbanizing at over 45% per annum, China's emerging cities are attracting retailers looking to tap into the burgeoning middle-class spending power. Company CEOs and marketers looking at cities such as Tianjin, Wuhan, Nanjing, and cities in lower tiers face a dearth of local data when planning their retail and distribution networks. Learn how your company strategists can develop bulletproof expansion strategies. When entering China's lower tier cities, don't take chances, make choices.
