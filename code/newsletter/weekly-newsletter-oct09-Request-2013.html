What

We’d love to meet you at ICSC in Hong Kong next week. Send us an email to set up a meeting.

Who & Why

The Reposition to Realize Potential Conference brings together the region’s government officials, real estate investors, developers, managers, consultants, retailers, and suppliers to discuss how the retail real estate community participates in building thriving cities with economic success. Also, listen to what experts have to say on the latest retail trends and changing consumer profiles as the industry scrambles to squeeze more and bigger returns from their real estate assets.

Where & When

Tower 5, 3 Tong Tak Street
Tseung Kwan O

Tuesday, October 15
