Targeting Malls in China Seminar Summary
A quick recap of what happened.

Tor Petersen of eno provided great insight on developing a brand in China. We learned from his mistakes of choosing the wrong locations and what to look out for in terms of expanding store networks. Additionally, Tor's views on evaluating site selections with regard to building brand visibiliy and generating profit are valuable lessons for growing in China.

James Grigsby of getchee pointed out that there isn't a magic bullet to store level success and it's critical to know where you are in the store network expansion curve. Most importantly, learn over time and always seek multiple data inputs.

Jonas Burstedt presented an inside look of Inter IKEA Center Group's strategy for developing shopping malls in China. Two case studies of their upcoming mall openings in Wuxi and Beijing highlighted their key performance indicators for targeting profitable mall locations outside the retail and business centers in the downtown city areas. Their knowledge and insight presented strategical drivers for mall developments.
