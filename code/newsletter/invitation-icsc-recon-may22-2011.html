Address Expansion Problems over Breakfast
Discuss retail development and management strategies.
 
Bruce Berkman, CEO of getchee, will facilitate a breakfast roundtable where he will bring up the difficulties of expanding a business in China. Bruce will also open up the doors to people who have problems with managing a network of stores. There will be four scenarios to choose from to create a valuable discussion.

Scenario 1
I have 10 stores. A majority of them are making a profit. Should I open more stores? If so, how many stores should I open and where? How do I expand efficiently?

Scenario 2
I'm rolling out a line of products. I want to sell them in my own stores and other shops. How do I efficiently manage distribution? How do I market the products and track campaign success?

Scenario 3
I manage a network of 20 stores. Five of the stores are not performing well. How do I pinpoint the causes of these underperforming assets? What corrective steps do I take?

Scenario 4
I manage a network of stores in the States. I want to expand in other markets like China, India, and Vietnam. How do I find the path of least resistance?

Look for Bruce Berkman's table under the Sustainability or Development category on Monday, May 23, 2011 from 630am-830am in the North Hall, Room N1.
